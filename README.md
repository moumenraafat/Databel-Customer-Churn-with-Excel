## ðŸ“‚ Dataset Details
The analysis is based on a comprehensive dataset containing customer-level information:
* **Customer ID:** Unique identifier for each client.
* **Demographics:** Age, Senior Citizen status, Under 30 status, etc.
* **Account Info:** Contract types (Monthly vs. Yearly) and billing details.
* **Churn Status:** Churn Label (Yes/No).
* **Churn Drivers:** Specific reasons for leaving (e.g., Competitor, Price, Dissatisfaction).
* **Geography:** State-level distribution.

---

## ðŸš€ Key Features
* **KPI Cards:** Instant visibility into Total Customers, Churned Customers, and Churn Rate %.
* **Reason Analysis:** Bar charts identifying the primary drivers of customer exit.
* **Demographic Breakdown:** Churn trends segmented by age groups and seniority.
* **Geographic Heatmap:** A color-coded map showing regional churn severity by state.
* **Interactive Slicers:** Dynamic filters for Contract Type, Gender, and State to allow for granular exploration.

---

## ðŸ’¡ Key Insights
* **The Competitor Factor:** Offers from competitors are the **#1 reason** for churn.
* **Age Vulnerability:** Customers **under 30** and **seniors** exhibit significantly higher churn rates.
* **Contract Risks:** Monthly contract holders are far more likely to leave than those on yearly plans.
* **Regional Hotspots:** Specific states show churn rates exceeding **60%**, signaling a need for localized marketing interventions.
